# Content Requirements

Owner confirmation is still required for:

- Exact confirmed services.
- Exact service-area cities.
- Service radius, if applicable.
- Public address policy.
- Hours.
- Pricing policy.
- Booking process.
- Credentials.
- Insurance or bonding status.
- Training methodology.
- Age or vaccination requirements.
- Class capacity.
- Cancellation policy.
- Real testimonials.
- Approved photographs.
- Social-profile URLs.
- Google Business Profile URL.
- Emergency and safety procedures.

Store structured status in `content/business_profile.toml` before publishing new service or location pages.
