# Local SEO Checklist

- Confirm exact business name for Google Business Profile and website.
- Confirm one consistent public phone number.
- Confirm whether a customer-facing address should be shown or whether the profile should be configured as a service-area business.
- Choose primary and secondary GBP categories.
- Confirm the service list and wording.
- Approve a concise business description.
- Confirm hours or decide not to publish hours on the site.
- Set appointment/contact URL to `https://cooper-and-co.com/contact`.
- Use `https://cooper-and-co.com` as the website URL.
- Add owner-approved photos with usage rights.
- Create a review-request process that follows platform rules.
- Respond to reviews consistently and professionally.
- Post class announcements when they are current and owner-approved.
- Add UTM parameters to GBP website/contact links if analytics is configured.
- Avoid duplicate business profiles.

Do not create or modify a Google Business Profile automatically from this repository.
